The Starbucks Pull

Hunter Sparrow

Just now·4 min read

By: Hunter Sparrow https://github.com/HunterKane/Starbucks-Reward-App-Capstone-Project

Starbuck has become the world’s top coffee retailer and holds a labyrinth of secrets. Did you know, the average customer visits Starbucks 6 times per month while loyal customers go 16 times per month. Now image all 30,000 locations around the world with this kind of customer base!

I could not resist, so I took a sip out of Starbuck’s data and discovered these interesting things.

What gender visits Starbucks the most?

I though it would be a closer percentage between genders. But, males won with a 58.1% customer base. A 16.2% higher customer rate than females.

What are the average age ranges of customers?

No surprises as generally young adults start drinking coffee which stays a preferred beverage thoughtout adults and elderly. Let’s break down the age groups to be a little more specific.

The data revealed the top 20 age ranges of Starbuck’s customers are from 48–68 years old. Somewhat surprising as I thought it would have been a younger crowd ranging from 20–35 years old.

The average age of Starbucks customers turned out to be 62.5 years old.

What kind of offers does Starbucks send out that are used?

Starbucks likes to send out 3 distinct offers. Buy One Get One (BOGO), Discounted Prices, and Informational.

The data shows BOGO to be the most popular. I can see why as you can invite someone along with you on your Starbucks trip. According to the data Starbucks sent out 71,617 BOGO that were used(total of 143,234 drink were made).

Second, the Discounted Price on Items for 69,898 counts. And, lastly Informational with 26,066 counts completed.

According to the data what were the most popular items sold?

Two products standout the most. The orange bar and the brown bar ranked among the highest. Both products were used with over 5,000 counts.

Let’s combine what we know so far and dig a little deeper

What age and promotion type are the most completed?

  • The adult age group has the highest response rate. Around 47,000 for BOGO and 45,000 for Discounted offer types.
  • The elderly are the second most frequent users. Around 25,000 for BOGO and 23,000 for Discounted offer types.

What offer type are completed by gender?

Males complete more offers at Starbucks

  • BOGO with 3,500
  • Discounted 3,300
  • Informational 1,600

Females competed these offers

  • BOGO with 2,400
  • Discounted 2,200
  • Informational 1,200

Starbucks is not considered the place for the cheapest coffee and I suspect Starbuck customer’s have a high income range. After analyzing the income status of Starbucks customers I found out the following.

What are the income ranges for genders that complete the most offers?

Initial Hypothesis: I suspect that people with higher income (generally older poeple who have developed thier career) will be the the most frequent customers to starbucks.

  • This to be mostly true as the ages 50–80+ have a higher average with an average income of $65,404. The data also showed older age ranges to complete the most offers.
  • On the lower scale we have ages 11–39. These age group correlate to lower reward offers and less offers completed.

Starbucks is located in 76 different countries and has an endless wealth of data created every moment to be analyzed and explored.

**Note about Data and Analysis

The data used was a dummy dataset and does not accuratly represent Starbucks. This was intented for education purposes to complete a project.
